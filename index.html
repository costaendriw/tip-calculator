Tipping Guide for 2025

Restaurant Service

  • 15% - Acceptable service
  • 18% - Good service (standard)
  • 20% - Great service
  • 25%+ - Exceptional service

Special Situations

  • Large groups: 18-20% minimum
  • Buffet: 10-15%
  • Takeout: 0-10%
  • Delivery: 15-20% (minimum $3-5)

Other Services

  • Bartender: $1-2 per drink or 15-20%
  • Barber/Hairstylist: 15-20%
  • Taxi/Uber: 10-15%
  • Hotel staff: $1-5 per service
