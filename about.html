About QuickTip Calculator

What is QuickTip?

QuickTip is a free, fast, and easy-to-use tip calculator designed to help you calculate tips and split bills with ease. Whether you're at a restaurant, bar, or any service establishment, our calculator makes tipping simple and accurate.

Why We Built This

We noticed that most tip calculators online were either:

  • Cluttered with too many ads
  • Slow and unresponsive
  • Difficult to use on mobile devices
  • Overcomplicated with unnecessary features

We wanted to create something different - a clean, fast, and mobile-friendly tool that does one thing exceptionally well: calculate tips.

Key Features

  • Fast & Simple: Calculate tips in seconds with an intuitive interface
  • Bill Splitting: Easily divide the total among multiple people
  • Mobile-Friendly: Works perfectly on smartphones and tablets
  • Preset Options: Quick access to common tip percentages (15%, 18%, 20%)
  • Custom Tips: Enter any tip percentage you prefer
  • 100% Free: No registration, no hidden fees, completely free to use

How It Works

Our tip calculator uses a simple formula to calculate your tip and total bill:

  1. Enter your bill amount
  2. Select your desired tip percentage (or enter a custom amount)
  3. Specify the number of people to split the bill
  4. Instantly see your tip amount, total bill, and amount per person

Privacy & Data

We respect your privacy. QuickTip Calculator:

  • Does not collect or store your personal information
  • Does not track your calculations
  • Does not require registration or login
  • Works entirely in your browser

All calculations are performed locally on your device. No data is sent to our servers.

Supported By Ads

QuickTip is completely free to use and is supported by non-intrusive advertising. We carefully select and place ads to ensure they don't interfere with your experience while using the calculator.

Feedback & Suggestions

We're constantly working to improve QuickTip. If you have suggestions, feature requests, or encounter any issues, we'd love to hear from you.

Your feedback helps us make QuickTip better for everyone.

Browser Compatibility

QuickTip works on all modern browsers including:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Opera

It's also fully compatible with iOS and Android mobile browsers.
